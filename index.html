About HIV/AIDS

HIV is a virus that attacks the immune system, which is our body's natural defence against illness. If left untreated, HIV can lead to AIDS (Acquired Immunodeficiency Syndrome). However, with early diagnosis and effective treatment, people with HIV can live long, healthy lives.

Our Services

  • Free HIV testing and counselling
  • Access to HIV medication
  • Support groups for people living with HIV/AIDS
  • Education and awareness campaigns
